About Me

Engineer by profession, artist by passion

Born and raised in the Canadian Capital, Ottawa, I currently work as a Verification Engineer. My experience in the tech industry has equipped me with a versatile skillset such as test automation in Python, knowledge in fiber optics and hardware, and technical document writing.

Growing up, I have always had two passions which complement one another: science, and traditional art. I am an enthusatic reader on topics about physics, astronomy, mathematics, and neuroscience. Occassionally, I will re-visit math and physics practice problems to maintain those skills. In contrast, I am an avid oil-painter bringing sci-fi and fantasy landscapes to life on the canvas. Other personal interests include reading sci-fi novels, yoga, salsa dancing, and learning Spanish.
